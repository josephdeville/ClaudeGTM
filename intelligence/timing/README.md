> **When do companies buy? What signals indicate readiness? What triggers urgency?**

## Purpose

**Bad GTM:** Spray and pray, hope something sticks

**Good GTM:** Target companies showing buying signals

**Great GTM:** Know *exactly* what triggers indicate a company is ready to buy, and why

## Core Principle

**Key Question:** "What events cause a company to say 'we need better GTM systems NOW'?"

## Timing Intelligence Categories

### 1. Funding Events

**Hypothesis:** Companies that just raised money invest in GTM infrastructure

**Intelligence to Capture:**
- How long after funding do they buy? (30 days? 90 days?)
- Which funding stages care? (Seed? Series A? Series B?)
- What's the trigger narrative? ("We just raised $XM to scale sales...")
- Which roles get hired post-funding? (VP Sales = buying signal?)
- Hit rate by funding stage?

**Pattern Format:**
```
Trigger: Series A Funding Announcement
Buying Window: 30-90 days post-announcement
Decision Maker: VP Sales (usually hired in first 60 days)
Trigger Narrative: "We need to scale from 10â†’100 accounts/month"
Hit Rate: [X]% convert to qualified opp
Time to Close: [Y] days average
Evidence: [Z] engagements tracked
```

### 2. Hiring Signals

**Hypothesis:** Hiring specific roles indicates GTM investment

**Key Roles:**
- VP Sales / CRO (they need infrastructure fast)
- Sales Ops / Rev Ops (they're building systems)
- SDR team expansion (need automation to scale)
- GTM leadership from larger companies (bring enterprise expectations)

**Intelligence to Capture:**
- Which roles are highest-signal?
- How long after posting do they buy?
- What's their first 90-day playbook? (Can you insert yourself?)
- Where do they hire from? (Enterprise background = compliance needs?)

### 3. Technology Adoption

**Hypothesis:** Companies adopting certain tools need GTM infrastructure

**High-Signal Tool Adoptions:**
- Moving from HubSpot Free â†’ Professional (ready to invest)
- Implementing Salesforce (enterprise systems need)
- Adding Outreach/Salesloft (scaling outbound)
- Deploying ABM platforms (sophisticated GTM motion)

**Intelligence to Capture:**
- Which tool changes predict buying behavior?
- What's the implementation timeline? (When do they need help?)
- What gaps do these tools create? (Clay fills what holes?)

### 4. Product/Market Signals

**Hypothesis:** Product milestones trigger GTM investment

**Potential Triggers:**
- Product launch (need to fill pipeline)
- New market expansion (need ICP research)
- Upmarket move (enterprise systems required)
- Geographic expansion (new data sources needed)

### 5. Performance Gaps

**Hypothesis:** Missed targets create urgent buying behavior

**Signals:**
- Layoffs in sales team (restructuring = new systems)
- Leadership changes (new VP = new stack)
- Public struggles (negative reviews, churn rumors)
- Competitive pressure (losing deals to better-automated competitors)

**Intelligence to Capture:**
- Which pain points create urgency?
- What's the trigger conversation?
- How fast can you close when pain is acute?

### 6. Seasonal Patterns

**Hypothesis:** GTM investments follow predictable cycles

**Patterns to Track:**
- Q4 budget planning (lock in next year)
- Q1 implementation (new fiscal year)
- Mid-year adjustments (missed targets â†’ investment)
- Summer slowdowns (avoid?)

## Building the Timing Playbook

### Phase 1: Hypothesis
Document what you *think* might be buying signals

### Phase 2: Tracking
Monitor these signals in your outreach
Tag opportunities by trigger type

### Phase 3: Validation
After 10-20 engagements, calculate:
- Conversion rate by trigger
- Time to close by trigger
- Deal size by trigger

### Phase 4: Optimization
Double down on high-signal triggers
Ignore low-signal noise
